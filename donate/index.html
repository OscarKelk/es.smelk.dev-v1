Community Outreach

Community outreach involves providing professional services, or services of a specific expertise, to a group of people who may not otherwise have access to those services. It is performed where those in need are located.

The Smelk Foundation

50% of donations paid to the Smelk Foundation are paid in full to homeless people displaced by Smelk offices and affiliated places of business.

The other 50% of donations are split up as follows:

  • 25% goes to administration costs
  • 25% goes to John Smelk (there's no way of getting around this he has to take his share)
  • 26% goes to building new Smelk offices in low-cost areas
  • 24% goes to building new Smelk-affiliated places of business. This includes:
    • Smelk coffee shops
    • Smelk banks (dealing exclusively in SMD)
    • Smelk debt collectors
    • Smelk tax offices
    • Smelk bagelries, and much much more.
